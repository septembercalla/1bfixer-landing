NOVA is a mobile application that connects customers with qualified home repair professionals. Submit a request, get matched instantly, and track your repair from start to finish.

How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Describe Your Problem

Take a photo or describe the issue. Our AI assistant analyzes the problem and provides an initial assessment with estimated costs.

2

Get Matched

We match you with a qualified professional in your area. Review their profile, accept the offer, and schedule a convenient time.

3

Track & Pay

Follow your repair in real-time. Receive updates when the technician is on the way, review the estimate, and pay securely through the app.

Notifications

Stay informed at every step

Users receive transactional SMS and email notifications to stay updated on their service requests. These are not marketing messages — only essential updates about active jobs.

Job Accepted

Notification when a professional accepts your service request.

Appointment Updates

Reminders and schedule changes for upcoming appointments.

Technician Arrival

Real-time notification when your technician is on the way or has arrived.

Payment Confirmation

Receipt and confirmation after deposit or final payment.
