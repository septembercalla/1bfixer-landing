Features

Why NOVA

AI Photo Analysis

Instant Matching

Transparent Pricing

How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Describe Your Problem

Take a photo or describe the issue. Our AI assistant analyzes the problem and provides an initial assessment with estimated costs.

2

Get Matched

We match you with a qualified professional in your area. Review their profile, accept the offer, and schedule a convenient time.

3

Track & Pay

Follow your repair in real-time. Receive updates when the technician is on the way, review the estimate, and pay securely through the app.
