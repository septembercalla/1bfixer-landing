Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

Nova (operated by Khudyna LLC) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Nova mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect user information such as name, email address, phone number, physical address, and service request details in order to provide home repair services through the Nova mobile application.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to match you with qualified home repair professionals, process service requests, facilitate payments, and communicate important updates about your service.

SMS Notifications

Your phone number may be used to send service-related SMS notifications including technician assignment, appointment reminders, schedule changes, technician arrival updates, and service status notifications. These are transactional messages related to active service requests only.

Message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared with assigned service professionals solely for the purpose of completing your service request.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission and secure data storage.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@1bfixer.com.
