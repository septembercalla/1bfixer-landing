Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Nova, operated by Khudyna LLC, respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Nova mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, service address, account details, uploaded photos, and service request information in order to provide our home services marketplace.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to create and manage your account, match you with qualified service professionals, process service requests, facilitate payments, improve the user experience, and provide support.

SMS Notifications

Your phone number may be used to send service-related SMS notifications, including technician assignment alerts, appointment reminders, arrival updates, service status changes, and payment confirmations. These messages are transactional and relate only to active service requests.

Message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Email Communications

We may send service-related emails, including confirmations, receipts, estimate updates, account notifications, and support communications.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary information with assigned service professionals and trusted service providers only to operate the platform and complete your requested service.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information, including secure data transmission and access controls.

Your Choices

You may update certain account information through the application. You may also opt out of transactional SMS by replying STOP, though doing so may affect important service updates.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@1bfixer.com.
